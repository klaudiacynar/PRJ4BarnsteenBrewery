Our Vision

In a world of differences, we believe that people should unite and celebrate diversity because only then can we truly live in the moment.

Our brand is centered around bonding and enjoying the experience. We want to create a diverse environment where community feel and playfulness are abundant.

Values

PASSION

We really believe in our product and what it will do for our community. We can relate to our customers which ignites a passion in us to do our best to bring people together.

COMMUNITY

We are inspired to create a safe environment for unique individuals who are willing to share their culture and experiences.

DIVERSITY

The majority of beer companies only cater to men. We want people of all genders, religions, and races to feel included in our product. Everyone should be able to enjoy our beer regardless of who they are.

Our Product

Beer description: Craft beer tailored to your experience. Each glass of our beer shows the innovativeness and enthusiasm of the drink experience and the intricacy of its ingredients. Craft brew is prized by millions who consider it to be not only a drink, but rather something to be shared, respected and appreciated.

Ingredients: Grain (mostly malted barley but also other grains), Hops, Yeast, and Water.

Our Team

AMBER DAM

Amber is the founding entrepreneur for this brand. She is all about living in the moment and experiencing new things in life. Her hobbies include dancing, painting and beach walks. She is really enthusiastic about Barnsteen Brewery, with the name actually being named after her. Barnsteen is Amber translated into Dutch!

KLAUDIA CYNAR

Klaudia is the founding entrepreneur for this brand. She is all about chill and genuine moments. She is down to earth and enjoys getting to know all types of people. Her hobbies include graphic designs, drawing and stargazing. She represents the more laidback part of our vision and is the brain behind our promotions.

Brand Identity

We want to emphasize that the best moments can be small things, like getting to know someone while watching a sunset, sitting in a park with friends oer going out. We want our audience to feel this when they are drinking our beer and bonding with people.

We want to be all about community and bonding with people. Our brand emphasizes the experiences we have with all people.
